## Master template

> Candid documentary photograph of [SPECIFIC ETHNICITY + age] [frontline worker in real uniform/setting], [doing a real small action — usually glancing at their phone], [real environment softly blurred behind]. Natural/window light, slight 35mm film grain, shallow depth of field, authentic and not posed. No text, no logos, no watermark.

**Always:** name a specific ethnicity per image (vary across a set: Black, Latina, South Asian, East Asian, Middle Eastern, mixed-race), real working environments, film grain, hopeful/dignified mood, phone-in-hand motif.

**Avoid:** studio strobes, glass offices, suits at laptops, "professional," "premium," handshakes, fake smiles to camera, gradient/white-void backgrounds, stock energy. **Banned clichés:** lightbulbs, hourglasses, network nodes, glowing brains, ladders to success. **Brand colour for any printed/UI props is Signal Orange `#FF5A1F` — never green/emerald (that's GigGrab).**

**No-text guardrail (append verbatim):** `No text, no logos, no watermark, no lettering anywhere in the image.`
